About our salon

Our experienced professionals use the latest techniques and products to deliver outstanding results. Each treatment is tailored to your needs—whether for skin revitalization, deep relaxation, or a peaceful escape from daily life.



Luminique is more than a beauty salon – it’s a community built on trust, quality, and care for your well-being.
